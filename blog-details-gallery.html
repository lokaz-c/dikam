DTC: Dikam Training Center

  • home
  • Blog
June 01, 2024 Lorenzo Kamanzi

Dikam Fashion wear's training Center, empowering women in learning new skills and getting Jobs.

In the heart of Rwanda, DIKAM Fashion Wear is making significant strides towards empowering women and fostering job creation through its innovative training programs. The company's latest initiative, the DIKAM Training Center (DTC), is designed to provide valuable skills and secure employment opportunities for women, particularly young mothers and youth.

DIKAM Fashion Wear, already renowned for its high-quality apparel, has launched the DIKAM Training Center (DTC) to further its mission of community empowerment. The idea for the training center emerged when the company was tasked with training teenage mothers in garment sewing. Recognizing the unique challenges faced by these young women, DIKAM's owner envisioned a supportive environment where the mothers could learn while their children were cared for in an on-site nursery. This holistic approach ensures that the mothers can fully concentrate on acquiring new skills without worrying about their children's safety and well-being.

At the end of the training program, participants are guaranteed employment at DIKAM, providing them with a stable income and a path to financial independence. This initiative not only uplifts the individual women but also contributes to the broader economic development of the community.

Collaborations and Contracts: Expanding the Impact

The DIKAM Training Center has already attracted significant partnerships aimed at expanding its reach and impact. One such collaboration is with the Young Women’s Christian Association (YWCA), supported by USAID. Through this partnership, DTC has secured a contract to train 80 teenage mothers, equipping them with essential garment-making skills. This program is a beacon of hope for these young women, offering them a chance to rebuild their lives and secure a brighter future for their families.

In addition to the YWCA partnership, DTC has also entered into a new contract with Rwanda's Ministry of Sport and Art. This agreement focuses on training 150 youth in garment manufacturing using cutting-edge technology. By integrating modern techniques into the training curriculum, DIKAM ensures that the participants are well-prepared to meet the demands of the contemporary job market. This initiative not only addresses the pressing issue of youth unemployment but also fosters innovation and technological advancement within the local textile and garment industry.

A Vision for the Future

The DIKAM Training Center exemplifies the company’s commitment to social responsibility and community development. By providing training and job opportunities, DIKAM is playing a crucial role in empowering women and youth, fostering economic growth, and promoting the "Made in Rwanda" movement. The success of DTC’s programs highlights the potential of such initiatives to transform lives and contribute to a more inclusive and prosperous society.

As DIKAM Fashion Wear continues to expand its training center and forge new partnerships, it sets a powerful example of how businesses can drive positive change. The future looks bright for the women and youth benefiting from DIKAM's training programs, and their success stories will undoubtedly inspire others to follow in their footsteps.
